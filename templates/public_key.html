How Digital Signatures Work

  • Signing: Files you upload are signed with your private key.
  • Verification: Anyone can verify using your public key.
  • Integrity: Detects any tampering with file contents.
  • Authenticity: Proves the file originated from you.

  2. They verify that your uploaded file’s signature matches this key.
